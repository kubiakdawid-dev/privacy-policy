Privacy Policy

Fascinating Mathematics • Developer: STORM Mobile Development Beauty Salon
Effective date: December 23, 2025

This policy applies to the mobile game “Fascinating Mathematics”.

Overview

STORM Mobile Development Beauty Salon built the Fascinating Mathematics app as a game. This page explains how we handle information when you use the app.

Important note: Fascinating Mathematics is designed to work without requiring you to provide personal information. We do not ask you to create an account.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email through the app.

The game may store basic progress (for example: levels completed, scores, or settings) locally on your device to make the game work as expected. This information does not identify you and is not sent to us.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party services that collect data from you (such as advertising networks or analytics providers), unless this is explicitly added in a future update.

If we ever add third-party services, we will update this Privacy Policy to describe what is used and why.

Internet / Online Features

Fascinating Mathematics does not require an internet connection for core gameplay. If your device is connected to the internet, the app still does not transmit personal data to us.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not request personal data, we generally do not receive information that could identify a child (or any user).

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will address the request.

Security

We value your trust. Because we do not collect personal data through the app, there is no user account data stored on our servers. Any game progress saved by the app is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device’s security measures.

Your Choices

  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored game data.
  • You can manage device permissions in your phone settings (the game does not require sensitive permissions to function).

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. The “Effective date” at the top will be updated accordingly.
